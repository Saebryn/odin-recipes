Cowboy Casserole

Description

A cheesy, hearty meal for the family! The best casserole because there are no green beans.

Also, so much cheese!

Serves 8-10

Equipment:

  • Deep-Dish Baking Pan
  • Medium size Frying Pan

Ingredients:

  • Tater Tots
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Large can of Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 Can of Whole Kernel Corn
  • 2 lbs. Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425*F
  2. In a medium size frying pan, brown 2 lbs. of ground beef
  3. Once browned, add one can cream of mushroom soup and one can corn
  4. Mix evenly and then bring to a simmer
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring regularly
  6. Add spices to taste
  7. Transfer to deep-dish baking pan and spread evenly
  8. neatly place tator tots over the whole surface
  9. Place in oven at 425*F for 25 minutes
  10. Shred cheddar cheese
  11. After 25 minutes, take out the pan, cover the tator tot surface with cheese
  12. place back in the oven for 10 minutes
  13. After 10 minutes, remove pan from oven, let cool and serve
  14. Enjoy!
