Mashed Potatoes


Description

A creamy, buttery American Favorite! From Thanksgiving to Christmas to a regular Sunday night, mashed potatoes are good any time!

What makes these potatoes different is that they are blended via hand mixer with the skins included, giving a consistent distribution of flavor while retaining texture.

Serves 8

Equipment:

  • Large pot
  • Hand mixer
  • Knife
  • cutting board

Ingredients:

  • 16 Potatoes
  • 2 sticks of Butter
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • garlic salt or powder
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps:

  1. Set butter out to warm
  2. Scrub potatoes
  3. Dice potatoes into evenly sized cubes and place them in a large pot
  4. Rinse the diced potatoes with water
  5. Fill pot with water until potatoes are covered by 1 inch of water
  6. Put pot over high heat and bring water to a boil
  7. Boil potatoes until cubes can be easily crushed by pressing against the side of the pot
  8. Drain water
  9. Add butter and milk to potatoes
  10. Use a hand mixer on low-med speed to blend the potatoes into a smooth consistency
  11. add salt, pepper, garlic to taste
  12. Enjoy!
