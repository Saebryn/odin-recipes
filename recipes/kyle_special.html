Kyle Special


Description

Its Clam! Its Chowder! Mix it with rice and you got yourself a great meal!

serves 1-2

Equipment:

  • Can opener
  • Rice Cooker

Ingredients:

  • 1 Can New England clam Chowder
  • 1 cup rice
  • Optional Salt, Pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook 1 cup of rice in a rice cooker
  2. Heat clam chowder in a small pot or microwave
  3. Add spices of you like!
  4. Combine and enjoy!
